I am Yuekun Yao, a postdoctoral researcher at [The Ohio State University](https://cse.osu.edu/people/sun.397), where I work with Prof. [Huan Sun](https://cse.osu.edu/people/sun.397). I completed my Ph.D. in the [Department of Language Science and Technology](https://www.uni-saarland.de/en/department/lst.html) at [Saarland University](https://www.uni-saarland.de/en/home.html), advised by Prof. [Alexander Koller](https://www.coli.uni-saarland.de/~koller/).
 <!-- Before that, I earned an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Edinburgh and a B.S. in Computer Science from East China Normal University. -->

My research asks how neural networks learn and generalize tasks. 
Lately, I study the limitations of transformers, and how data, training recipes, and model architectures shape these behaviors. 
Areas I am interested in include interpretability, LLM knowledge, and reasoning. 
Before that, I worked on semantic parsing, with a focus on out-of-distribution and compositional generalization.
